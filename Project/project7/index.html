WHERE ICONS ARE BORN

Welcome to The Official Guide to Knicks History, a study guide for New York Knicks fans! You can find the Knicks’ greatest players in team history on the IconKnick players page and a timeline of the greatest moments in Knicks history on the IconKnick moments page.

The Knicks aren’t just a team, they’re an integral part of New York City's culture. From the team's two championships in the 1970s to its recent resurgence, the Knicks' story is full of legendary moments made by New York sports icons.

This site is designed to help you experience the moments that shaped the team’s identity and the players who turned Madison Square Garden into the Mecca of Basketball.

Take your time exploring. When it comes to Knicks history, there’s always another piece to the puzzle.

ABOUT THE NEW YORK KNICKS

The Knicks were one of the 11 original NBA franchises that played in the league’s inaugural season in 1946-1947. The team’s two championships came in 1970 and 1973, and they have five other NBA Finals appearances. The team currently plays at Madison Square Garden in Midtown Manhattan, and its rivals include the Boston Celtics, Philadelphia 76ers, and Miami Heat.

WHY SHOULD I WATCH THE CURRENT KNICKS?

After reaching the Eastern Conference Finals for the first time in a quarter century in 2025, the Knicks have championship aspirations heading into the 2025-2026 campaign. Under new Head Coach Mike Brown, the team is expected to play with a fast-paced, team-oriented offensive scheme.
